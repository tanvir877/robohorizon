Our Journey

From a small research team to a global leader in robotics innovation, explore the key milestones that have shaped our company's history.

2015

Company Founded

RoboTech Nexus was founded by Dr. Alexander Chen and Dr. Sophia Williams with a vision to revolutionize robotics technology. Initial funding secured from venture capital firms specializing in deep tech.

2017

First Research Breakthrough

Development of our proprietary neural network architecture for robotic vision systems, achieving 95% accuracy in complex object recognition tasks, setting a new industry standard.

2019

Global Expansion

Opened research facilities in Tokyo and Berlin, expanding our international presence and establishing partnerships with leading universities and research institutions worldwide.

2021

Industry Recognition

Awarded the prestigious Global Robotics Innovation Award for our work on autonomous drone swarm coordination systems, beating competitors from 27 countries.

2023

Major Acquisition

Acquired SoftGrip Technologies, integrating their revolutionary soft robotics technology into our product line and expanding our capabilities in delicate object manipulation.

2025

Next Generation Launch

Launched our next-generation AI-powered manufacturing systems, now deployed in over 50 factories worldwide, increasing production efficiency by an average of 32%.
