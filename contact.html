Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our research and collaboration opportunities.

How can I collaborate on research projects?

We welcome collaboration opportunities with academic institutions, industry partners, and individual researchers. Please contact us with your proposal and area of interest.

Do you offer internship programs?

Yes, we offer internship opportunities for students and recent graduates in robotics, AI, and related fields. Check our careers page for current openings.

How can I access your research papers?

Our published research papers are available through our online repository. Some papers may require academic or institutional access.

Can I visit your research facility?

We organize facility tours for potential collaborators and academic groups. Please contact us to schedule a visit with your specific interests.
